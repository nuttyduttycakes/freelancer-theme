Flavours

Nutty Dutty Cakes are all hand made with the finest ingredients. There is nothing like the classic flavours, such as vanilla sponge cake and carrot cake, but I love to experiment with unusual flavours or try pairings that are outside the box!

Cake flavour ideas

  • Vanilla sponge
  • Carrot
  • Carrot and orange
  • Chocolate
  • Chocolate hazlenut
  • Chocolate orange
  • Almond
  • Coffee
  • Lemon
  • Pistachio
  • Lemon and rosemary

Cake toppings and fillings can simply be a flavour infused sugar syrup or a vanilla buttercream and jam. They can however be any flavour you desire. I love chocolate and honeycomb, so crumble honeycomb into a chocolate buttercream to give it a little crunch. If you prefer a more fruity taste a simple buttercream mixed with strawberry or blueberry tastes amazing on a vanilla sponge or cupcake.

Topping flavour ideas

  • Blueberry
  • Chocolate honeycomb

The lists are endless! If there is a flavour you love or fancy trying please challenge me!
