About

It all started when my little boy was born and I promised I would make his birthday cake every year from scratch. The first year I improvised and spent most of the night before frantically trying to put something together. Although it was slightly stressful at the time I loved designing and making something special for him and seeing his little face light up when he saw it for the first time. It gave me the bug!

I then made cakes for friends and family and after a while I decided to take the plunge and that is how Nutty Dutty Cakes began.
